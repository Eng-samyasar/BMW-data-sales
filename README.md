# Overview

This analysis summarizes BMW sales data (2010–2024) highlighting top models, regions, fuel types, and pricing.

# Top Models & Profit

- 7 Series, i8, X1 are top-selling models.  
- 7 Series is the most profitable.  
- Peak yearly profit in 2024 by X6.

# Regional Sales

- Europe: i8  
- Asia: X1  
- North America & Middle East: 7 Series  
- South America: X6  
- Africa: 5 Series

# Fuel Type Insights

- Hybrid leads in sales (25.5%) and profit (~4.82T USD).  
- Petrol & Electric follow closely; Diesel slightly lower.  
- Hybrid dominates most regions; Petrol preferred in Middle East & South America.

# Color & Transmission

- Red is the most popular car color.  
- Manual: 50.3%, Automatic: 49.7%.

# Engine & Price

- Engine sizes are balanced.  
- Minimal correlation between engine size, mileage, and price.  
- Median price ~$74–76K; total range $30–120K.  
- Region has minor impact on price.

# Summary

BMW’s sales and profit patterns show consistent model performance, stable pricing across regions, and clear preferences in fuel type and color.
